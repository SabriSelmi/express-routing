Recette d'aujourdhui: PIZZA MARGHERITA

Previous Next

STEP 1

Preheat oven Th8 (240 ° C).

STEP 2

Season the tomato puree with salt, 1 tbsp. tablespoon oil and pepper.

STEP 3

Drain the mozzarella and cut it into small cubes.

STEP 4

Using the rolling pin, clean and floured, spread the pizza dough on parchment paper suitable for a mold about 30 cm in diameter. Make a bead all around.

STEP 5

Spread the tomato puree over the pizza and put it in the preheated oven Th8 (240 ° C) for 10 min.

STEP 6

Take the pizza out of the oven, spread out the mozzarella cubes and bake it again for about 10 minutes.

STEP 7

Take the pizza out of the oven, season with remaining oil and basil leaves.
